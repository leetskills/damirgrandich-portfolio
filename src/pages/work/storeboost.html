Background

Boosting store footfall with DOOH

Store Boost connects live footfall data with Programmatic Digital Out of Home to deliver targeted messaging to potential customers nearby.

The Cloud + Compass' proprietary footfall tracking technology enables stores and restaurants to analyse their store's current footfall and trends over time and compare these with the local area.

Get footfall when you need it with data driven marketing

Using a Programmatic DOOH bidder, stores can run advertising on the digital panels around them and measure the impact it has on their footfall in-store.

Takeaways

Keep It Simple, Stupid ðŸ’‹

The design process I used reflected the simplicity of the solution.

Kicking off the design sprint with super lo-fi collaborative sketching allowed a rapid development of ideas before going into the high-fidelity visual design stage.

I made sure that the handover to the engineers was as easy as possible by building part of the front-end for them, freeing them of the pain of getting the UI layout to spec.

I made sure everything in the design could be built, because, well, I'd actually built it.

The fully responsive prototype's HTML & SASS code was of a high enough standard that the engineers could literally copy and paste. The charting library was fully implemented.

Everything was in place so that the engineers could focus on the important things: building the functionality.
