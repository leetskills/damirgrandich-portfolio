Project

Pollpass Chat Bot

Built from the ground up to be a transparent and GDPR-compliant way of collecting research data directly from a variety of digital consumers.

With the increasing difficulty in passive tracking-based data collection, active collection methods such as surveys have become vitally important.

It has historically been difficult to attract a representative set of users to spend 30 minutes completing a market research survey.

Traditional users of market research surveys skew heavily toward students and full-time parents. With Pollpass, we targeted a wider demographic by appealing with personality and delight.

Challenge

The chat had to feel engaging for any duration

Minimising cognitive load was a key objective in order to keep things interesting and reduce fatigue.

As users would be completing lengthy surveys with numerous questions—some of which can be wordy and repetitive—UI elements were kept to an absolute minimum.

A design mockup doesn't tell you anything about how an app would feel after using it for an extended period of time, so I built a prototype that could.

Outcomes

A prototype allowed me to simulate a real chat

With a data-driven HTML/CSS/JS prototype I was able to test all manner of survey questions, from the short and snappy to the wordy and repetitive.

I could see how it felt to spend an extended amount of time answering questions: to see where fatigue might set in, and where to inject some variety as required.

Timings became super important. Transitions and animations were carefully tweaked until everything felt right.

I was able to rapidly iterate on ideas with quick and dirty guerilla testing. Unexpected solutions uncovered themselves in ways they mightn't have from static mockups.
